LANY

Lany is a alt/indie band originating from Nashville, Tennesse.

Band Members

Paul Jason Klein

Born in Tulsa, Ohio the 33-year old singer/guitarist fronts the band as the lead vocalists and lyricist.

Leslie Priest

Serving as the guitarist/background vocalist for LANY

Jake Goss

Jake Goss is the drummer for LANY and hails from Nashville, Tennessee where the band was orginally formed.
